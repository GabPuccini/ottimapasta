Our story

Still the same family, still the same welcome

Ottima Pasta began with one family kitchen in 1935, and it is still owned by the family who started it. Our look takes its lead from the hand painted Italian shop fronts that have always drawn people in.

Step inside and it should feel like your Nonna's kitchen table. Honest food, generous hospitality and a warm, simple welcome for everyone who walks through the door.
